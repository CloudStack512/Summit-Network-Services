How a typical project works

  1. Quick call or email. You describe the current setup and text or email a few photos of your rack/closet.
  2. Rough estimate. We provide a ballpark based on number of racks, runs, and services needed.
  3. On‑site visit. We confirm scope, finalize the quote, and schedule the work.
  4. Cleanup & testing. We perform the agreed services with minimal disruption to your team.
  5. Documentation delivery. You receive a simple PDF packet summarizing what was done and how everything is labeled.
